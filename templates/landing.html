1) Purpose of the Study

This study investigates how people interact with online news articles and the topics they are interested in. The goal is to better understand how news content is perceived in digital environments.

2) What Will You Be Asked to Do?

After an introductory questionnaire, you will go through 6 interactive rounds where we show you different news article together with recommended articles.
In each round, you will be asked to answer a few questions about your opinions and impressions on the articles. The study will take approximately 12 minutes to complete.

3) Data Privacy and Your Rights
  • Your participation is voluntary and you may withdraw at any time without giving a reason.
  • All data collected in this study will be treated confidentially and used only for research purposes.
  • Your responses will be stored and processed in anonymized form.
  • Any data published or shared will be fully anonymized and cannot be traced back to you.
