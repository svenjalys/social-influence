1) Purpose of the Study

This study explores how people interact with online news articles.

2) What Will You Be Asked to Do?

We will show you four different news articles and choose a recommended article in each round. We will ask you some questions before and after.

3) Data Privacy and Your Rights
  • Your participation is voluntary and you may withdraw at any time without giving a reason.
  • All personal data will be kept confidential and used only for the purposes of this study. Further processing of anonymized data may occur if it aligns with the study's goals. Any data published or shared with others outside the research team will be fully anonymized and untraceable to you.
